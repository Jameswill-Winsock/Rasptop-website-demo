ABOUT THE PRODUCT

Key features of our new launch

Customisable

Our line of RaspTops are highly customisable, with each order built to the user's choice, including your choice of processor. This means you can get them in a big clamshell case, or a small USB PC.

Repairable

Being built from commonly market available parts ensures your purchase is repairable down to the smallest parts with large documentation. You can get it repaired from us, or from anyone else, and your warranty will not go away.

Tailored for every use

For battlefield use, to a on-the-go watch, or a simple USB you can plug into your TV, the RaspTops can be used for any need. Simply ask, and we are happy to help.

Support

Our 24/7 customer service ensures you have all the after sales service you require. We also ensure most applications run on our device, and port them to the architecture if necessary. If there is no support for your favorite app, message us!

We know design.

Who likes ugly, boring looking products? The answer: Nobody. We know how to make hardware that works great, and still gets the damn job done with style.

WHY US

Let's be real. There are a million options out there. Why buy us?

Open source

In today's world, where everything is closed source, and where code and ideas are stolen left and right, we choose to release all our code for everyone to see. Go ahead, dig through the code. You'll find no spyware.

Long term support

Manufactures who drop support for their devices right after a few years deserve a special place in technology's Hall of Shame. We firmly believe that devices should have long term support for atleast 20 years, so we support all architectures we sell, be it x86, ARM, RISC-V etc.

Armed to the teeth

Want more USB ports? Gigabit ethernet support? A ridiculously powerful GPU built-in? We have your back. Each order is customised as per your wishes, and we can do it in bulk too. Just simply *ask*.

Pricing

We have transparent pricing, even for the wires we put in. Any add-ons you request, will also have their pricing displayed. Community requests for popular software to be ported shall be free of cost.

Specifications
